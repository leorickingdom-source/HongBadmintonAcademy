HONG BADMINTON ACADEMY — UPDATE 2026-06-30 (part 2)
The rest of the big upgrade: students, attendance, leave & make-up,
monthly reports, finance, and Chinese language.

WHAT'S NEW
==========

1. STUDENTS
   - Nickname field — shows next to the name everywhere.
   - "Assigned coach" per student (the coach responsible for them) — and you
     can filter the whole directory by coach.
   - Admin can set a student's training level (L1–L6) directly on the edit
     form. Any change is logged and the parent gets a notification on a
     level-up, same as a promotion.

2. ATTENDANCE & COACHES
   - Coaches now check THEMSELVES in ("I'm here" button) — so you can see
     who actually showed up to teach.
   - New "Coach coverage" screen (last 30 days): per session — did the coach
     check in, and did they mark the roster?
   - Wrong attendance mark? It can now be fully cleared/undone (coach and
     admin).
   - Filter classes and students by coach across the system.

3. LEAVE & MAKE-UP CLASSES
   - Parents request leave on any upcoming session from their app (with a
     reason), and can withdraw it while pending.
   - New "Leave & Makeup" screen for admins: approve or decline. Approving
     automatically marks the child EXCUSED for that day.
   - You can book a make-up class in the same step — the child then appears
     on the coach's check-in list for that make-up session automatically.
   - Coaches can request leave for their own session too; you approve and
     then cancel or re-assign the class.
   - Parents get an app notification + push for every decision.

4. MONTHLY REPORT (back, and separate from exams)
   - Coaches grade their WHOLE class once a month in one screen:
     Fitness / Skills / Attitude (1–5) + a short comment. Every tap saves.
   - Parents see a new "Monthly Report" tab: month by month — attendance %,
     average session rating, the coach's three marks, the comment, and
     reward points earned.
   - Exams stay on the Progress Card, untouched.

5. FINANCE
   - Invoices past their due date now automatically flip to "overdue" every
     night (they used to stay "unpaid" forever).
   - The admin dashboard now shows money at a glance: Collected this month,
     Outstanding, and Overdue — respecting the branch you're viewing.

6. 中文 (CHINESE LANGUAGE)
   - Parents can switch their app to Chinese: Account → Language → 中文.
   - Translated so far: the parent menu, home page, schedule (including the
     whole leave-request flow) and the Monthly Report.
   - English stays the default; each parent chooses for themselves.

7. SECURITY HARDENING
   - Branch walls extended to every day-to-day record (attendance, marks,
     check-ins, leave, monthly marks) — a branch admin cannot reach another
     branch's data even by talking to the server directly.
   - Push notification setup now available to every admin (was accidentally
     super-admin only), covering leave requests and payment alerts.
