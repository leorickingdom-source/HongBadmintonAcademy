HONG BADMINTON ACADEMY — SYSTEM UPDATES (Part 2)
Date: 17 June 2026
Covers everything done since the previous update note.

====================================================================
ANALYTICS — MAJOR UPGRADE (academy KPIs)
====================================================================
- New headline KPI row: Revenue, Collection rate, Active students,
  Attendance rate, Retention rate, Average skill.
- Average skill is now clear: shown as a % with an easy "≈ X / 10"
  read-out, plus the change vs last month (improvement/decline).
- Skills breakdown: average score per skill (e.g. Smash, Footwork,
  Backhand, Serve) — actionable for coaches.
- Retention block: 30-day retention rate, average attendance per
  student, and students who haven't shown up in 30+ days.
- Coach performance table: students, attendance %, and average skill
  score given — per coach.
- Class occupancy: how full each class is vs its capacity.
- Fee aging: outstanding money grouped by how overdue it is
  (0-30 / 31-60 / 61-90 / 90+ days).
- Student rank distribution now shows percentages, not just counts.
- New-student trend (6 months) and an inactive/"left" count.
- Reward leaderboard is now tied to the month you're viewing.
- Everything above is included in the PDF and CSV exports too.

(Not built — these need new data we don't yet record: rank-progression
rate, true churn/renewal rate, tournament participation.)

====================================================================
WHATSAPP — FASTER & CLEARER
====================================================================
- Announcements, the monthly notice, and report-card notices now send
  IMMEDIATELY (one group message, instant) instead of sitting in the
  slow, throttled queue. If the worker is offline they fall back to the
  queue automatically.
- Cancelling a session now notifies the affected parents immediately.
- Congratulations message: when a student is promoted to a higher rank,
  their parent automatically gets a WhatsApp congrats.
- Monthly post personalisation: admins can save a free-text note that is
  added to the automatic monthly reports + fees post — so it goes out as
  one warm, personalised message each month.
- The Announcements page is now ONE composer with two clear buttons:
  "Send now" and "Save as monthly note" (no more two look-alike boxes).
- Re-link the WhatsApp number from inside the app: Settings now shows a
  QR to scan after a logout — no server/SSH access needed. (The worker
  also gained a browser QR page for the same purpose.)

====================================================================
HOLIDAYS
====================================================================
- One-click cleanup now also removes already-scheduled upcoming sessions
  that fall on a holiday (past/cancelled sessions are kept).

====================================================================
SETTINGS — SIMPLIFIED
====================================================================
- Monthly schedule reduced from three fields to two: "Monthly run day"
  (when invoices + reports generate and the combined post goes out) and
  "Invoice due day". Existing settings carry over automatically.
- Removed the admin self profile-edit card to declutter Settings.

====================================================================
HOUSEKEEPING & DATA
====================================================================
- The daily backup now automatically trims old history (WhatsApp log +
  finished message queue older than 90 days) AFTER taking the snapshot —
  keeps the database lean; nothing is lost (it's in that day's backup).
- Invoices can now be searched by invoice number.
- Leaderboard "attendance tier" was clarified and then removed (kept the
  attendance rate, streak and class rank).

Note: WhatsApp sending is currently switched OFF (worker paused). Resume
it in Settings and re-link the number (Settings → scan QR) when ready to
go live.
